Mark Carney

Leader of the Liberal Party

A Vision for Canada

Mark Carney is a distinguished economist and statesman with a proven track record of steering financial systems through global challenges. Since becoming Leader, he has focused on fiscal stability, economic growth, and social progress.

Under his leadership, the Liberal Party is advocating for a "Family Pack" of policies designed to support the middle class, invest in education, and ensure that Canada remains a leader on the world stage.

"We are not just a party of the past; we are the party of Canada's future. Together, we will rise up and build a country that works for everyone."
