Our Policies for 2010

Bold ideas for a better Canada. Explore our comprehensive platform for the 2011 election cycle.

The Family Pack

Our flagship policy designed to support the heartbeat of Canada: the middle-class family.

  • Family Care Plan: A $1 billion investment to help families caring for elderly or disabled relatives.
  • Learning Passport: Providing every high school student with $4,000 ($6,000 for low-income) for post-secondary education.
  • Early Learning & Childcare: Re-establishing a national framework for affordable, high-quality childcare.

Economic Growth & Fiscal Responsibility

Leading Canada out of the recession with smart investments and a plan to return to surplus.

  • Corporate Tax Reform: Cancelling further corporate tax cuts to invest in infrastructure and education.
  • Clean Tech Investment: Creating a multi-billion dollar fund to support renewable energy and green manufacturing.
  • Infrastructure Renewal: A 10-year plan to modernize Canada's transit, roads, and bridges.

Environment & Climate Change

Taking real action on climate change while building the economy of the future.

  • Cap-and-Trade System: Implementing a market-based approach to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Home Retrofit Program: Incentives for Canadians to make their homes more energy-efficient.
  • National Water Strategy: Protecting our most precious resource from pollution and over-extraction.
